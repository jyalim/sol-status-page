Key: the squares above illustrate distinct compute nodes and their status on the Sol supercomputer. A square's color reflects the node's state as labeled by the legend in the upper-right. Hovering the mouse over a square will provide additional details about that compute node, such as features (job constraints), partition info, and QOS info. Some nodes have Graphical Processing Units (GPUs), which are indicated as idle or allocated by an empty (○) or filled (●) symbol.
